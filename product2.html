Human Resource Management System

HR Manager is designed to process and provide all information starting from recruitment till final settlement. This solution has been developed with extensive HR domain knowledge under consultation of experienced HR Managers. The architectural design of the software is highly flexible which can be customized according to the users requirements.

Key Features

  • Payroll administration and export
  • Benefits management
  • Expenses management
  • Document management
  • Attendance tracking (sickness and holidays)
  • Time logging
  • Performance evaluation
  • Recruitment and applicant tracking
  • Onboarding
  • API functionality
  • Data importing
  • Reporting
